Bull Network Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20/11/2023

Thank you for using Bull Network! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User Data: When you sign up or log in, we collect and store your basic account information securely in an encrypted form in our database.

1.2 Device Cache: To enhance user experience, we may utilize device cache for rapid data loading. This cache is used to store temporary data and is not permanently stored on our servers.

2. Use of Information

2.1 User Data: We use your encrypted user data to facilitate account management, authentication, and to provide personalized services within the app.

2.2 Device Cache: The device cache is used to improve the speed and efficiency of data loading within the app. It does not involve the permanent storage of personal information on our servers.

3. Events and Bull Networks

3.1 Events: Bull Network provides events for users to joinued. Participation in these events is voluntary, and users may choose to participate or not.

3.2 Bull Networks: Users can complete events to earn Bull Networks. These tokens may be used within the app for various purposes as described in the app's functionalities.

4. Security

We take the security of your information seriously. All user data is stored in encrypted form, and we implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of your information.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review our Privacy Policy periodically.
